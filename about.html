Four people with a
Dream

Ethan Greene, Sydney Wertz, Trevor Redding, and Madison Dabbs came together to want to help people. Seeing the state of the economy, where nearly 80 percent of Americans can not comfortably afford a home, something needed to be done. Hence, Dukedom was born, a way to break people into the real estate market, without breaking the bank.
